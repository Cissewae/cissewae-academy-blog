According to the UNICEF, Quality education is a global goal that aims to empower people with knowledge, skills and values for a better future.

It involves providing access, equity, inclusion and lifelong learning opportunities for all, especially the most marginalized and vulnerable.

Quality education also requires improving the learning outcomes, teaching methods, curricula and assessment systems in schools and beyond.

Despite some progress, millions of children and youth still lack quality education due to poverty, conflict, discrimination and other barriers.

Quality education is essential for achieving other sustainable development goals, such as reducing poverty, promoting health, fostering peace and protecting the environment.

Quality education is a human right and a public good that benefits individuals, communities and societies.
